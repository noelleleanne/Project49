Interesting Facts about Chameleons

  • They mainly change colour in order to communicate or regulate body tempetature.
  • Unlike many lizards, Chameleons can't regrow their tails.
  • Their eyes can swivel around in two different directions simultaneously.

Interesting Facts about Pigeons

  • Pigeons mate for life, and tend to raise two chicks at the same time.
  • Pigeons are highly sociable animals. They will often be seen in flocks of 20-30 birds.
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities. They can detect sounds at far lower frequencies than humans are able to, and can thus hear distant storms and volcanoes.

Interesting Facts about Pandas

  • Pandas have vertical slits for pupils which is different from normal bears.
  • They have an extended wrist bone that they use like a thumb to help them grip food.
  • Giant pandas spend 10-16 hours a day feeding, mainly on bamboo.

Interesting facts about cicadas

  • Most have red-orange eyes. But occasionally cicadas have blue, white or grayish eyes.
  • They improve lawns by digging tunnels that allow air into the soil. When they die, their rotting bodies put nitrogen into the soil.
  • The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus.
